FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE, MUFFINS CITY, 2013/04/01

YayPonies get a new identity!

Hello everypony, today we can announce a complicated decision. We have decided to stop G4 support. You probably know that YayPonies only support the best quality, and after Twilight's Alicornication, it became impossible to deliver something good enough for us.

Actually, the G4 website is still available while we complete the new website; as you can see we still don't have the complete collection of old-gen ponies.

Speaking of which, if you have VHS or DVD rips for those ponies, contact us at yayponies@tormail.org, we're interested!
