  1. Where are the MLP:FIM Episodes?

    Actually, MLP:FIM Episodes are still available on the old "G4" website.

  2. What player or software should I use?

    VLC Media Player is able to play every format on all major platforms. Additionally, the CCCP Codec Pack with Media Player Classic gives great results on Windows. On Linux you can use mplayer or smplayer (check your package manager). On Mac OS X, MplayerX gives good results!

  3. What's up with the filename format?

    On YayPonies we have lot of different ponies coming from different source. Our filenames, even if looking a bit cryptic, give all the information you need about the file you downloaded. Here's how to read it:

    YP-G1D-01x01 : YayPonies-G1 DVD Rip-Season 1 Episode 1
    YP-G2Y-01x01 : YayPonies - G2 Youtube rip (Coming from another source) - Season 1 Episode 1

    etc...

  4. Is there a way to get faster direct download from hosting companies without them being premium?

    It looks like you can download from filecloud at a good speed if you create a free account and if you copy files on your account before downloading them.
