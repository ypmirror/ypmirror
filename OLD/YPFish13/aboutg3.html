Welcome to Yay Ponies! Yay Ponies was started shortly after the takedown of PonyArchive by Hasbro, Inc. This was a second initiative as an attempt to getting the episodes of My Little Pony: Friendship is Magic out there in various formats.

After Twilight's Alicornication, we decided to get My Little Pony classic episodes instead of Friendship is Magic.

Our episodes of My Little Pony are hosted on a server somewhere in Ponyland.

WE ARE NOT THE SAME STAFF THAN PONYARCHIVE ONE, ITS USELESS TO CONTACT THEM ABOUT US!

Supporting Us

At the present moment, we will not accept donations. Simply put, we won't accept donations for a service provided by the main corporation. You can, however, help us by providing us your TV / DVD / VHS rips. Or, if you are an encoder, you can also get in touch with us! Contact us on PonyChat.net's or on Canternet.org's' IRC at the channel : #YayPonies.
